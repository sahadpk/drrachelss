About Dr.Rachels

Dr. Rachel Ommen is an alumnus of Christian Medical College, Ludiana and then completed her MD(Internal Medicine) from KVG medical college, Sullia. Further went ahead to pursue her post doctoral fellowship in Rheumatology centre which caters to more than 200 patients per day. During her training in CARE she was posted in the Rheumatology ward, trained in musculoskeletal ultra. She then joined Travancore Medical College, Kollam in 2019 as Consultant Rheumatologist and continues to work there. She has quite a few publications and has special interest in the management of SLE Rheumatoid and arthritis spondyloarthritis . She is actively involved in various activities to increase awareness and educate about rheumatic diseases among the general public and also the medical fraternity.
