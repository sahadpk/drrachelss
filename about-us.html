About Dr.Rachels Rheumatology centre

Rheumatologists are Doctors Who Treating Diseases

Dr. Rachel has worked hard to build a medical paradise focused exclusively on diagnosing and treating all types of arthritis and autoimmune related conditions. Combining clinical skills with technology. She strives to provide comprehensive world class cost effective treatment for the various rheumatic diseases using a multidisciplinary approach with security and compassion. Personalised unrushed care is a hallmark of the practice and each patient is treated like a valued member. Patient education and training provides to view their as partners in improving health, wellness and overall quality of life also forms an integral part of practice. She also believes in integrating rheumatology with alternative system of medicine to provide the best of care to all patients with rheumatic diseases
